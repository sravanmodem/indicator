Indicator Interpretation Guide

SuperTrend

Green = Bullish trend, Red = Bearish trend. ATR-based trend following indicator.

RSI

<30 = Oversold (Buy), >70 = Overbought (Sell). Momentum oscillator.

ADX

>25 = Strong trend, <20 = Weak/No trend. +DI > -DI = Bullish.

MACD

Histogram > 0 = Bullish momentum. Crossovers signal trend changes.
